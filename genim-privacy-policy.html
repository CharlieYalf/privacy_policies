Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Genim handles your information. We prioritize your privacy while providing powerful AI image description capabilities.

Information Collection and Use

We collect and use the following information:

  • Authentication data through Supabase (email, encrypted password)
  • Transaction records for billing purposes
  • Temporary image data during processing

Authentication

We use Supabase for user authentication. Their privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy. We only store the minimum required information for account functionality.

AI Processing

Image processing is handled through fail.ai. Images are:

  • Processed temporarily for description generation
  • Not permanently stored
  • Not used for training or other purposes

Data Storage and Security

We maintain minimal data storage:

  • Authentication data (managed by Supabase)
  • Transaction records
  • No permanent storage of processed images
  • No storage of generated descriptions

Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not handle or store any payment information. All payment processing is subject to Apple's privacy policy and terms of service.

Disclaimer of Liability

While we strive for accuracy, we are not responsible for any inaccuracies in AI-generated descriptions. Users should review and verify descriptions before use.

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell user data
  • Share data with third parties (except as required for service provision)
  • Use data for marketing purposes

Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • Supabase (authentication)
  • fail.ai (AI processing)
  • Payment processors

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data handling practices.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via:

  • Our contact form
  • GitHub
