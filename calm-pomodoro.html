About the App

Calm Pomodoro is a minimalist productivity timer that helps you maintain focus and take regular breaks. With its clean interface and gentle notifications, it's the perfect companion for your work or study sessions.

Features:

  • Clean, distraction-free interface
  • Customizable work and break durations
  • Gentle notification sounds
  • Dark mode support
  • Session statistics
  • No ads or tracking
  • Complete privacy - all data stays on your device
