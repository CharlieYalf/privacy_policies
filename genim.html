About the App

Genim is an innovative AI-powered tool that generates detailed, accurate descriptions of images. Whether you're optimizing for accessibility, creating content, or enhancing SEO, Genim provides intelligent, context-aware descriptions.

Features:

  • Advanced AI-powered image analysis
  • Detailed, context-aware descriptions
  • Perfect for accessibility compliance
  • Ideal for content creators and SEO specialists
  • Complete privacy - no account required
  • All data stored locally on your device
  • Simple, intuitive interface
  • Fast processing times
