About the App

Photo Clutter is a simple yet powerful tool for organizing and cleaning up your photo library. Easily identify and remove duplicate photos, organize your images, and reclaim storage space on your device - all while maintaining complete privacy.

Features:

  • Identify and remove duplicate photos
  • Smart organization suggestions
  • Storage space analysis and optimization
  • Batch operations for efficient cleanup
  • Complete privacy - everything processed on-device
  • No account required
  • Simple, intuitive interface
  • Fast scanning and processing

Your Privacy Matters

Photo Clutter is built with privacy at its core. All photo processing happens directly on your device, and no photos are ever uploaded to external servers. Your memories stay private and secure.
