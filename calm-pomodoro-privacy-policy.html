Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Calm Pomodoro handles your information. We prioritize your privacy and have designed our app to be completely private and secure.

Information Collection and Storage

Calm Pomodoro is designed with privacy in mind:

  • All timer settings and statistics are stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No personal information is collected
  • No analytics or tracking systems are implemented

Data Storage

All app data, including:

  • Timer settings
  • Session statistics
  • App preferences

is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Notifications

The app requests notification permissions solely to alert you about timer completions. These notifications are processed locally and do not involve external servers.

Third-Party Services

Calm Pomodoro does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms. The app functions completely offline after installation.

App Store Data

The only information collected is standard App Store analytics provided by Apple, which is not linked to your identity or content.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from users of any age, including children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
