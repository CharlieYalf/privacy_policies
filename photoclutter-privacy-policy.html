Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Photo Clutter handles your information. We prioritize your privacy while providing powerful photo organization and cleanup capabilities.

Information Collection and Use

We collect and use the following information:

  • Photo data that you choose to process within the app
  • Transaction records through Apple's App Store (if applicable)

Local Processing and Storage

Photo Clutter is designed with privacy as a priority:

  • All photo processing happens directly on your device
  • No photos or metadata are uploaded to external servers
  • All app data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not maintain any external databases or user accounts

Photo Access

Photo Clutter requires access to your photo library to function. We handle your photos with care:

  • Photos are only accessed when you use the app
  • We never transfer your photos off your device
  • Any changes to photos (deletion, organization) are performed only with your explicit permission

Data Storage and Security

We maintain minimal data storage:

  • All app data is stored locally on your device
  • Photo metadata for organization purposes is stored locally only
  • Transaction records (handled by Apple, if applicable)

Payment Processing

All payments (if applicable) are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not handle or store any payment information. All payment processing is subject to Apple's privacy policy and terms of service.

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Collect user accounts or credentials
  • Transfer photos to external servers
  • Sell user data
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use data for marketing purposes

Third-Party Services

Our service only integrates with:

  • Apple Photos API (for accessing your photo library)
  • Apple App Store (for payment processing, if applicable)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data handling practices.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via:

  • Our contact form
  • GitHub
