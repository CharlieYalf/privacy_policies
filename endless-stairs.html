About the Game

Help Basil climb to the moon in this challenging arcade adventure! Master one-tap controls, unlock powers, and customize your character. Warning: addictively frustrating!

Features:

  • One-tap gameplay that's easy to learn, thrilling to master
  • Action queueing system for smooth, satisfying combos
  • Rich progression with upgrades and power-ups
  • Gorgeous visual effects and responsive controls
  • Customize Basil with unique cosmetics
  • Competitive leaderboards to challenge friends
  • Offline play for anywhere, anytime fun
